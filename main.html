S.A.F.E
Security Awareness & Fundamentals Education

Project Safe's Goal

To inform students how to manage, be safe, and recognize aspects of life and the internet. The course will go over topics that are not commonly touched upon in everyday schooling, however, it is relevant for anyone with a device that is connected to the internet. Most high school students have a good understanding on how to use a couple.

Course Objectives

1. To give an understanding of malware and how to avoid it (Social eng, BTBP, PHISH)
2. To give guidance on how to create strong passwords as well as manage them (PASSMAN, BTBP)
3. To educate on how personal data can be everywhere without knowing or consent
